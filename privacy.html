Privacy Policy

Data we collect

  • Email address — only if you create an account.
  • Message text you paste or scan for decoding.
  • Sender names you provide alongside messages.
  • Tone analysis results — tone label, stress score, suggested replies, real ask.
  • Usage patterns — timing, frequency, and communication patterns derived from your translations.

How we use it

Your data is used solely to:

  • Generate message translations and calm replies.
  • Build your personal pattern profile and history.
  • Send your weekly communication brief (if opted in).

We do not sell your data, share it for advertising, or use it to train external models.

Third-party processors

Messages you submit are processed by:

  • OpenAI API — your messages are sent to OpenAI's GPT-4o model for analysis. OpenAI does not use API inputs for training. OpenAI privacy policy →
  • OpenAI Vision API — when you use the camera/screenshot feature, images are sent to OpenAI's vision API for text extraction and are not stored beyond the API call.
  • Supabase — stores your account data, translations, and patterns with row-level security. Supabase privacy policy →

Data storage location

All data is stored in the EU region (Frankfurt / Zurich). If you use the app without signing in, data is stored only in your browser's local storage and never leaves your device.

Data retention

We retain your data for 12 months from your last activity. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time — deletion is immediate and permanent.

Your GDPR rights

If you are located in the EU or UK, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of your data.
  • Correct — update inaccurate information.
  • Delete — permanently remove your account and all data.
  • Export — receive your data in a portable format.
  • Object — to processing in certain circumstances.

To exercise these rights, email privacy@composurely.com.

Contact

Questions or data requests: privacy@composurely.com
