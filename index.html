How it works

Three outputs.
One tap.

Composurely analyses the message, the sender, and your context — then gives you everything you need to respond with clarity.

01

Paste the message

Copy any Slack message, email, Teams chat, or WhatsApp. Paste it exactly as you received it — don't summarise. Add optional context: what just happened before this arrived.

02

Get the real read

Composurely returns three things: what they actually want (stripped of emotional charge), a tone breakdown with the specific phrases that reveal the subtext, and a stress score.

03

Send the calm reply

A professionally composed reply — under 40 words, calm, clear, and ready to send. One tap to copy. Or make it shorter if you need to.

Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Built for daily use. Every feature earns its place.

Two lenses

See every message through a charitable read and a stress read. Understand both the best-case interpretation and the implicit pressure you actually felt.

Scan & screenshot

Can't copy-paste? Take a photo or upload a screenshot. Composurely extracts the text automatically using AI vision.

Pattern detection

After every 5 messages, Composurely surfaces patterns in how your colleagues communicate — timing, tone, and trigger situations.

Relationship map

A visual network of the people in your inbox, sized by message volume and coloured by stress level. See who's costing you the most energy at a glance.

Weekly brief

Every Monday, a plain-language summary of your communication week. Who sent what, when, and what it meant. No emotional language — just the facts.

Private by design

Your messages are processed securely. No message content is used for training. You can delete your account and all data at any time.
